# PES Reference Checklist

1. Does each citation support the exact claim in the sentence?
2. Are IEEE Xplore versions used when a preprint and final article both exist?
3. Are journal abbreviations official or at least recognizable?
4. Are standards cited by standard number and year?
5. Are datasets and software cited with durable identifiers when available?
6. Are references recent enough for smart-grid technologies whose standards,
   tariffs, or cyber-security practices change quickly?
7. Are foundational power-system methods cited when the manuscript depends on
   them, even if the implementation is new?
8. Are review papers used for background rather than as evidence for a specific
   numerical claim?
