# Power and Smart Grid Chart Patterns

## TPWRS

- Power flow, OPF, and operations: time series, convergence curves, dispatch
  stacks, constraint violation bars, and sensitivity heatmaps.
- Dynamics and stability: modal damping plots, frequency nadir curves,
  phase-plane plots, and disturbance timelines.
- Planning and reliability: cost-risk Pareto curves, reliability indices with
  uncertainty intervals, and scenario distributions.
- Resilience: event timeline plus restoration trajectory and energy-not-served
  quantification.

## TSG

- Microgrids and ADNs: feeder one-line diagrams, voltage profiles, control
  scenario comparisons, and DER hosting capacity maps.
- AMI and data analytics: missing-data matrices, forecasting error distributions,
  anomaly score timelines, and feature attribution heatmaps.
- DER/EV integration: charging profile comparisons, voltage violation box plots,
  transformer loading curves, and locational sensitivity matrices.
- Cyber-physical security: attack timeline, detection score, affected assets,
  and recovery performance.

## Common mistakes

- Using color alone to distinguish many scenarios.
- Plotting raw simulation traces without an engineering claim.
- Omitting units or per-unit bases.
- Mixing transmission-market evidence into a TSG paper without explaining the
  smart-grid scope connection.
