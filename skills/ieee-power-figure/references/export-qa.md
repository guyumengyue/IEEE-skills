# Figure Export QA

Run this checklist before delivery or submission.

1. The figure has a one-sentence claim.
2. The width is 3.5 in for one column or 7.16 in for two columns.
3. Axes have units, including p.u., MW, Mvar, kV, Hz, $, or seconds when needed.
4. Line graphs have unique markers and line styles.
5. The figure remains understandable in grayscale.
6. The legend does not hide data; direct labels are used where clearer.
7. Text is 7-8 pt for ordinary IEEE figures and not below 6 pt for ticks.
8. PDF or SVG is exported for vector graphics.
9. PNG or TIFF is exported at 600 dpi for line-art fallback.
10. Caption defines scenarios, sample counts, and error bars.
11. All generated data used for examples is labeled as synthetic.
12. No private path, attachment name, or local provenance appears in labels.
