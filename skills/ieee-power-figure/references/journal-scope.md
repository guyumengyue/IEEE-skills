# TPWRS and TSG Figure Scope

## IEEE Transactions on Power Systems

Use TPWRS framing for figures about:

- Power system modeling and analysis.
- Power flow, OPF, congestion, redispatch, and techno-economic analysis.
- Reliability, resilience, uncertainty, and stochastic optimization.
- Dynamic performance, stability, controls, and dynamic security assessment.
- Transmission and distribution operation, state estimation, and control.
- Generation, transmission, distribution, and integrated resource planning.

## IEEE Transactions on Smart Grid

Use TSG framing for figures about:

- AC/DC microgrids and active distribution networks.
- Multi-energy systems tied to smart-grid operation.
- Demand response and demand-side management.
- DER and EV interactions with power grids.
- Smart meters, AMI, smart sensors, and distribution PMUs.
- Peer-to-peer, transactive energy, and blockchain grid applications.
- Cyber-physical and cybersecurity applications for power grids.
- Data analytics and big-data applications to microgrids and ADNs.

## Boundary warning

TSG lists several transmission-only topics as out of scope, including pure
transmission system studies, transmission-system load or price forecasting,
transmission protection, WAMS/WACS PMU applications for transmission systems,
and economic or market framework issues that are not tied to DR/DSM, ADNs,
microgrids, DERs, EVs, or multi-energy systems.
