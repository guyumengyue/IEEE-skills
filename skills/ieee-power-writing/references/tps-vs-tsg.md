# TPWRS vs TSG Fit

## TPWRS

Best fit for:

- Power system analysis, modeling, computing, and economics.
- Dynamic performance, stability, and dynamic security assessment.
- Operations, security, state estimation, and control.
- Planning, expansion, resource planning, and reliability or resilience.
- Broad transmission or distribution system methods.

## TSG

Best fit for:

- Microgrids and active distribution networks.
- DER, EV, AMI, smart sensor, and distribution PMU applications.
- Demand response and demand-side management.
- Cyber-physical security for smart-grid operation.
- Transactive energy, peer-to-peer exchange, and blockchain grid applications.
- Data analytics tied to microgrids or ADNs.

## Boundary language

Use a scope sentence when needed:

- "The study focuses on active distribution network operation rather than
  transmission-level market clearing."
- "The PMU application is evaluated for distribution-system observability,
  which aligns with smart-grid monitoring rather than WAMS transmission control."
- "The data analytics task is tied to microgrid operation and DER coordination."
