# Abstract and Keywords

## Abstract checks

- One paragraph.
- About 150-200 words for PES Transactions.
- Self-contained.
- No references, footnotes, displayed equations, or tables.
- Avoid unexplained acronyms.
- Include objective, method, main evidence, implication, and boundary.

## Keyword checks

- Use three or four different keywords or phrases when possible.
- Prefer discoverable engineering nouns:
  - active distribution network
  - demand response
  - distributed energy resources
  - optimal power flow
  - phasor measurement unit
  - resilience
  - smart grid
  - voltage control
- Avoid repeating the exact same phrase too many times.
